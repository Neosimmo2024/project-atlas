# Product Principles

## Foundational Principles

1. The human always decides.
2. AI explains its recommendations.
3. Every business datum belongs to a tenant.
4. No sensitive information is exposed client-side.
5. A feature must reduce mental load.
6. Security and GDPR are designed from the start.
7. One Pull Request equals one coherent feature.
8. No development outside the sprint scope.
9. Every stable version is tagged.
10. A simple used V1 is preferred to a theoretical platform.

## V1 Commercial Scope

The commercial V1 must allow a real estate network to:

- centralize prospects and talents;
- attach each person to one or more organizations;
- track a recruitment relationship in a pipeline;
- record interactions and next actions;
- import contacts without creating duplicates;
- launch compliant email and SMS sequences;
- visualize daily priorities;
- measure conversions from sourcing to recruitment.

## Out Of Scope For V1

- Full property management.
- Real estate transaction management.
- Marketplace.
- Native mobile application.
- Advanced knowledge graph.
- Finance and accounting.

## Decision Rules

- Do not revisit a fixed decision without a blocking bug or an ADR.
- Fast profitability is preferred to exhaustive scope.
- Features outside V1 remain in the backlog.
- The product is tested first by NEOS IMMO.
- Unverified data is never presented as certain.
- Each new module must reinforce the main value proposition.
