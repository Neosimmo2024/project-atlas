# Product Roadmap

## Current Stable Baseline

`v0.3.0` is the first stable Project Atlas baseline and includes:

- Core Foundation;
- People;
- Organizations.

## Version Roadmap

| Version | Objective | Modules |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `v0.3.0` | Stable foundations | Core, People, Organizations |
| `v0.4.0` | Initial relationship graph | Relationships |
| `v0.5.0` | Operational tracking | Interactions, Tasks, Notes |
| `v0.6.0` | Complete recruitment workflow | Pipeline, stages, meetings |
| `v0.7.0` | Data entry | CSV import, advanced deduplication |
| `v0.8.0` | Communication | Brevo, email, SMS |
| `v0.9.0` | Automation | n8n, follow-ups, sequence stops |
| `v1.0.0` | Commercial product | Business dashboard, reporting, finalized security |
| `v1.1+` | Intelligence | AI scoring, summaries, recommendations |

## Target Business KPIs

- New talents: profiles added over a period.
- Qualification rate: qualified profiles divided by created profiles.
- Contact rate: contacted profiles divided by qualified profiles.
- Meeting rate: meetings divided by contacted profiles.
- Recruitment rate: recruited profiles divided by meetings.
- Average delay: time from sourcing to recruitment.
- Source performance: conversions by channel.
- Recruiter performance: conversions by owner.
- Avoided duplicates: blocked or warned duplicate creations.
- Late follow-ups: overdue unfinished tasks.

## Pre-Commercialization Checklist

- RLS tests actually executed with two tenants.
- Authenticated E2E tests executed.
- Backup and restoration documented.
- Monitoring and application logs in place.
- Legal notice, terms, DPA, and privacy policy.
- GDPR request management.
- Customer onboarding.
- Billing and subscription management.
- User support.
- Incident recovery plan.
