# Atlas Vision

## Purpose

Project Atlas exists to transform scattered information, complex professional relationships, and founder workload into a clear, reliable, and actionable system.

## Mission

Atlas helps each organization understand its people, relationships, decisions, and priorities, then automate useful actions without removing human control.

## Promise

Transform complexity into serenity.

## Product Vision

In the short term, Atlas is a vertical CRM for identifying, qualifying, tracking, and recruiting real estate talents.

In the long term, Atlas becomes an Enterprise Intelligence platform: an operational digital twin of the company, able to connect people, organizations, interactions, decisions, tasks, and knowledge.

## Positioning

| Item | Atlas Position |
| --- | --- |
| Current category | Multi-tenant real estate recruitment CRM |
| Target category | Enterprise Intelligence Platform |
| Differentiation | Relationship understanding, complete history, automation, and recommendations |
| First pilot customer | NEOS IMMO |
| Initial market | Agent networks, agencies, franchises, and real estate recruitment firms |
| Target business model | Monthly SaaS subscription by tenant and service level |

## Foundational Product Beliefs

- AI is not the product; it is an integrated capability.
- The heart of the product is the multi-tenant relational data model.
- Real estate is the first vertical, not the product limit.
- The software must create clarity before it creates automation.
- Humans always keep control over important decisions.

## Product Canon

Atlas should always give users the feeling that the system knows their activity, shows what matters, and helps them act without taking the decision away from them.

Guiding phrase: we build more serene companies.
